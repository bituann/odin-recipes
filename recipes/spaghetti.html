Jollof Spaghetti

Description

This recipe is a very simple one to do, one that you can do without breaking the bank. It makes use of easy to find ingredients, yet it comes out tasting so good. I hope you enjoy this as much as I did.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Groundnut Oil
  • Salt
  • Maggi
  • Curry
  • Spicity
  • Tomato Paste

Steps

  1. Put water in a pot and let it boil
  2. Add the spaghetti and let it cook until it's soft
  3. Pour the spaghetti into a sieve and let the water drain
  4. Put vegetable oil into the pot
  5. Add your tomato paste, then salt, and maggi
  6. Add a little heat to it, and mix it
  7. Add curry and spicity to it, then mix it again
  8. Keep mixing until it's well integrated with the oil
  9. Add the spaghetti to it, and stir until it's looking red
  10. Turn off the heat, and let it simmer for a minute
  11. Done!
